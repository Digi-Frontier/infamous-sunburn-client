HOW TO GUIDE

Before you try our Visualization Tool, here are some helpful hints to get you started.

Step 1 - Uploading a photo

By following these helpful hints, the visualizer will provide better results.
  • The house or building is in full view in the image.
  • Avoid cutting off part of the house or building.
  • The house or building takes up most of the image instead of the surrounding environment.
  • Trees or objects in front of the house may provide unrealistic imagery.

Step 2 - Siding options

Select from stucco or vinyl exterior siding options to visualize.

Step 3 - Color themes

Select from various color themes to visualize.

Step 4 - Visualize

The visualization process will apply to the entire image and we recommend trying more than one time with multiple scenarios of siding and colors.

We hope you find the results inspiring. Our team is ready to answer your questions.
